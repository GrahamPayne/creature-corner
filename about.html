Creature Corner

Creature Corner is a studio dedicated to handmade creatures, masks, artifacts, and impossible specimens. Every piece is designed, sculpted, painted, and finished by hand—exploring the boundaries between sculpture, illustration, and world-building.

The work draws from speculative biology, science fiction, natural history museums, and the idea that strange things exist just outside the edges of what we know. These are objects that feel recovered rather than made—museum specimens from an imagined history, biological anomalies, and relics from forgotten expeditions.

The Process

Each piece begins as a concept sketch or digital sculpture, then moves through 3D printing, traditional sculpting, moldmaking, casting, painting, and finishing. The goal is to combine digital tools with traditional craftsmanship—using technology to prototype and refine, but always returning to hands-on fabrication.

Materials include resin, silicone, epoxy clay, acrylics, oils, found objects, and whatever else the piece demands. Some works take weeks; others evolve over months.

Follow Along
